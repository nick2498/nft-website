What are goofy pigs?

The GoofyPigs is a collection of 5,555 unique pieces of collectible art, living on the Ethereum-compatible Polygon Network. Each Goofy Pig is a unique and virtually handmade art image. Each piece of art is digitally hand-drawn by artist Prerit Gupta. All Pigs have a variety of outfits, faces, hats, colors, and accessories.
